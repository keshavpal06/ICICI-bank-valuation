# Comparable Company Valuation — Indian Banking Sector
**ICICI Bank vs. Peers | Price-to-Book Analysis | July 2026**

## Objective
Benchmark ICICI Bank's valuation multiples against 5 peers to assess 
whether its P/B premium is justified by fundamentals.

## Data
Live data from Screener.in (July 1, 2026) across 6 banks:
ICICI Bank, HDFC Bank, Kotak Mahindra, Axis Bank, SBI, IndusInd Bank.

Metrics collected: P/E, P/B, ROE, Market Cap.

## Key Findings

### 1. Peer Comparison
| Bank | P/E | P/B | ROE |
|------|-----|-----|-----|
| ICICI Bank | 18.2x | 2.73x | 16.1% |
| HDFC Bank | 16.2x | 2.12x | 13.8% |
| Kotak Mahindra | 20.6x | 2.17x | 11.2% |
| Axis Bank | 16.0x | 1.95x | 13.1% |
| SBI | 11.4x | 1.59x | 15.4% |
| IndusInd Bank* | 81.6x | 1.11x | 1.36% |

*IndusInd excluded from median — distressed outlier due to FY25-26 
derivative accounting provisions.

**Peer median (ex-IndusInd): P/B 1.95x | ROE 13.1%**
**ICICI premium to median: P/B +40% | ROE +23%**

### 2. ROE vs P/B Regression
Running a linear regression of ROE against P/B across the 5-bank 
peer group (IndusInd excluded) yields R² = 0.03 — meaning ROE 
explains only 3% of P/B variance in this sample.

This low explanatory power is the central finding: P/B premiums 
in Indian private banking are not driven by current ROE alone. 
ICICI's 24.6% premium above its ROE-implied fair value (predicted 
2.19x vs actual 2.73x) reflects qualitative market pricing — 
asset quality improvement post-2018 cleanup, digital banking 
positioning, and management execution credibility — factors 
that ROE alone cannot capture.

### 3. IndusInd Outlier
IndusInd's ROE collapsed to 1.36% in FY26 due to derivative 
accounting restatements, while its P/B remains at 1.11x — above 
what a 1.36% ROE would justify. This suggests the market is 
pricing in ROE recovery, not current fundamentals.

## Methodology Notes
- P/B calculated as CMP / Book Value per share from Screener.in
- Peer median used over mean to reduce IndusInd distortion
- Regression run on 5 observations — insufficient for statistical 
  significance, presented as directional analysis only
- All data as of July 1, 2026 market close
